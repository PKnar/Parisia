Design Description

Idea

For this assignment we chose to make a Responsive Travel website for Paris.
Our goal for this website was to make it all in one in a way. To to do that
we decided to have cards for each destination and each card would include
buttons, that would allow the use to buy the ticket, book a taxi or check for
available transportation methods. Which would be useful for any traveler.

Target User

Even though this website could be used by any traveler.
We wanted to create something more. Having group travelers
in our mind we decided to build a group chat feature, in it,
where the trip organizer could add people, choose destinations
and post it on a feed etc.
Our initial target group would be school trip organizers. Paris is a common destionation for school trips , so having
everything in one place could be helpful for the trip leaders.

Group work

Considering the fact that we all were new to the prototyping we knew this assignment
would take a time. That is why, after coming up with ideas and making some sketches
we decided to divide the tasks between 3 of us.
Cleo, was assigned to put the sketches and ideas together and turn them into
higher quality Sketches, plus the Storyboard.
Thomas was assigned to do the Wireframes & Personas.
I (Knarik) was assigned to do the digital prototype & the presentation website.

All of us were actively involved in this assignment. There was no "I did more you did less", the tasks
were equally divided.Overall it was a nice experience to work with each other.
Even though each one of us did our part, we also were involved in each other's tasks through giving opinions/feedback.

Wireframe

Thomas was assigned to turn these sketches into Wireframes

In his own words: " My main job during this project was to make the Wireframe of the website. Although it at first seemed like a simple task, it turned out to be quite tricky. My group member, Cleo, made drawings of how the website would approximately look like and I had to make a digitalized detailed version of those drawings. First thing I did was too really analyze the drawings and brainstorm how I can make it look better. For the design I used a software called Pencil which was a very good choice. My mockup has a black and white design, and the grey boxes were to show where there would be pictures. I consulted this with my group members, and they were very happy with it. I also wanted to make sure that the mockup was not too detailed since it must be as simple as possible. This was very hard for me because I sometimes was not sure if it was too much or not. In my opinion this was a hard task especially for me because I am not a very artsy person. This forced me to come out of my comfort zone and do something I am not very good at. I learned a lot from doing the mockup and thankfully I had my team members supporting me all the way through. Next time I do a mockup, I will try to put in a little extra detail if possible and make it look a lot more appealing. "
